What Is Edexcel IGCSE?

Edexcel IGCSE (International General Certificate of Secondary Education) is an internationally recognized qualification designed for students between the ages of 14 and 16. It is offered by Pearson Education, a UK-based education company, and is widely recognized by universities and employers around the world.

The grading system for Edexcel IGCSE is based on a numerical scale of 9 to 1, with 9 being the highest grade and 1 being the lowest.

There are several benefits to having an Edexcel IGCSE certificate:

  • International recognition: Edexcel IGCSE is an internationally recognized qualification that is widely accepted by universities and employers around the world.
  • Broad range of subjects: Edexcel IGCSE offers a wide range of subjects, including core subjects like English, maths, and science, as well as languages, humanities, and business-related subjects.
  • Flexibility: Edexcel IGCSE offers flexibility in terms of the choice of subjects and the mode of learning. Students can choose to study full-time or part-time, and can take exams in different sessions throughout the year.
  • High academic standards: Edexcel IGCSE is known for its high academic standards and rigorous assessment. It is designed to prepare students for further education and for the demands of the workplace.
